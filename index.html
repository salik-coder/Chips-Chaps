Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery hours?+

We deliver every day from 11 AM to 1 AM, including public holidays.

Do you offer contactless delivery?+

Yes — just leave a note at checkout and our rider will leave your order at the door.

Can I customize my order?+

Absolutely — add notes at checkout for extra sauce, no onions, or any other request.

What payment methods do you accept?+

Cash on delivery, credit card, debit card, and PayPal are all supported at checkout.
