About ChipsChaps

From one grill to a city favourite — here's our story.

👨‍🍳
Our story

Fast food, done properly.

ChipsChaps opened its first counter with a simple frustration: why does "fast" so often mean "forgettable"? We set out to prove that quick service and genuinely good food aren't mutually exclusive — hand-pressed patties, double-fried golden fries, and sauces made in-house every morning.

Years later, we're still doing it the same way, just for a lot more people.

Our mission

Serve honest, crave-worthy fast food, fast — without cutting corners on ingredients or hospitality.

Our vision

To be the first name people think of when "fast food" and "actually good" belong in the same sentence, in every city we serve.
